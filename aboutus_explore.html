Welcome to Tulu Sahitya

Tulu Sahitya is a platform dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich literary and cultural heritage of the Tulu-speaking community. We strive to showcase the beauty and significance of Tulu language, literature, traditions, and customs.

Our mission is to create awareness and appreciation for Tulu Sahitya (literature) among a wider audience and to provide a platform for Tulu artists, writers, and enthusiasts to connect and collaborate.
